About Pharma Future

ABOUT OUR WEBSITE ?

A website to predict the resistance of antibiotics to save the time and resources of doctors,patient & a hospital.Thus providing fact,effective & prompt results while saving lives & ensuring better credibility of a certain hospital in the society.


How this website works ?

Consulting an experienced registered medical practitioner to create a database of Infection vs Antibiotics that is usually prescribed for it.The website collects details of all the admitted patient from their sample culture report,specifically the Resistance on the antibiotics the doctor has prescribed.

Making a another database from the data collected by website and assigning it's Infection/ Disease column from the first database by matching corresponding combination of antibiotics.Other factors include - age,gender,locality,pregency,immune status,outcome of treatment.

Applying ML on the last database to choose the best antibiotic in view of possible resistance.
